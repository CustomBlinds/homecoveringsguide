PVC Indoor Shutters for Knysna Homes

PVC shutters suit areas with high humidity, daily moisture changes and direct sunlight. They are designed for modern homes where owners want a clean look with good privacy, shade control and durability.

  • Resistant to moisture, swelling and coastal air.
  • Excellent for bathrooms, kitchens and full sun rooms.
  • Light control plus clean, minimal style.
  • Easy to wipe clean and maintain.

Security Shutters for Added Protection

Security shutters combine airflow, privacy and protection in one solution. These are suitable for sliding doors, patios and openings where homeowners want fresh air without compromising safety.

  • Aluminium construction built for strength.
  • Multi point locking for improved access control.
  • Allows ventilation while securing the home.
  • Excellent for coastal living where open windows are needed.

Choosing the Right Shutters for Knysna

When selecting shutters in Knysna, consider:

  • Moisture: PVC performs best in bathrooms, kitchens and coastal humidity.
  • Security: Use aluminium security shutters for patios, sliding doors and high traffic entries.
  • Louvre Width: Wider louvres give more modern aesthetics and better airflow.
  • Maintenance: Both options require minimal care compared to timber.

Installation Tips for Knysna Homes

  • Measure accurately for frames inside or outside the reveal.
  • Ensure walls around sliding doors are level for security shutter tracks.
  • Consider sun direction in Belvidere, Rexford and Simola where afternoon light is strong.
