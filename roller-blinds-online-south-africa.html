Roller blinds online in South Africa

Roller blinds are the most straightforward blind to buy online because they need only a width, a drop and a fabric choice. The fabric is the decision that matters: blockout for full darkness, light-filtering for soft daytime privacy, sunscreen to cut glare while keeping a view. This guide covers how to choose, what drives the price, and the no-drill option that suits renters and tiled or aluminium frames.

What to check before you order online

  • The blind is made to your exact measurements, not a fixed size.
  • It is manufactured in South Africa, so a remake is quick if needed.
  • There is a genuine fit guarantee covering a measuring slip.
  • Pricing is shown live as you enter your sizes.
