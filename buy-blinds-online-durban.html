Buying made-to-measure blinds online works well for Durban homes, provided you choose materials that suit the local climate and order from a supplier that manufactures in South Africa and stands behind the fit.

What Durban homes need from a blind

Durban's subtropical humidity is the deciding factor. Real timber blinds warp and the fittings on cheaper blinds corrode in the coastal damp, so the materials that last are aluminium venetians and plaswood (faux wood), both of which shrug off moisture, plus synthetic roller fabrics that do not hold damp. For humid Durban bathrooms and kitchens specifically, a moisture-resistant blind is not a luxury, it is the difference between a blind that lasts years and one that fails in a season.

Delivery and ordering in Durban

Choosing a moisture-tolerant material up front is the single best decision for the Durban climate.
