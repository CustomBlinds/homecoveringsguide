Buying blinds online in Cape Town

Buying made-to-measure blinds online works well for Cape Town homes, provided you choose materials that suit the local climate and order from a supplier that manufactures in South Africa and stands behind the fit.

What Cape Town homes need from a blind

Cape Town puts two specific demands on a blind. The south-easter drives hard against exposed windows, so for patios and wide openings a track-guided or weighted system holds far better than a loose blind that will flap. And the salt air near the coast corrodes cheap fittings, which makes stainless or properly treated hardware worth insisting on. Indoors, the strong Cape sun on north and west-facing rooms is where sunscreen roller fabric earns its place, cutting glare and heat while keeping the view of the mountain or sea.
