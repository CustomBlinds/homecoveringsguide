Why Plett homes lean on awnings

Plettenberg Bay has long, bright days and strong high-angle sun. Awnings extend the hours you can comfortably sit outside, protect sliding doors and soften heat in the rooms behind them.

  • Add shade where existing roofs stop too short
  • Protect stack doors and sliders from sun and rain
  • Lower heat gain into lounges and bedrooms behind glass
  • Create an extra “room” for relaxed summer living

Fixed wedge awnings

Fixed wedge awnings are simple, strong structures that always stay in place. They suit doors and windows that need permanent cover.

  • Great over braai doors and small patios
  • Reliable in wind when correctly engineered
  • Fabric or aluminium options depending on look and budget

Retractable and folding arm awnings

Retractable awnings are popular in Plettenberg Bay where you want shade in the day and open sky at night. Folding arm systems extend out from the wall then roll away neatly.

  • Good for flexible use over decks and dining areas
  • Can be motorised for easy control in coastal wind
  • Needs regular checks and good fixing into structure

Louvred awnings

Louvred awnings combine shade and airflow with the option of closing blades in rain. For coastal homes with mixed weather, they add a more architectural feel.

  • Blades tilt to follow the sun or close completely
  • Helps manage heat build-up on glass doorways
  • Often chosen for premium decks and entertainment areas

What to finalise before you request quotes

  • Measure rough width and projection you need
  • Note how much head height you can afford to lose
  • Decide if you want manual or motorised control
  • Photograph the area from inside and outside

FAQ: Awnings in Plettenberg Bay

Do awnings cope with Plett wind?
Yes, if correctly specified and mounted. It is important to use coastal-appropriate hardware and to follow the installer’s advice about when to retract awnings in strong wind.
Canvas or acrylic fabric?
Both are used. Coastal-rated acrylic fabrics often resist fading and mildew very well. Your installer can show swatches suited to Plettenberg Bay weather.
Can I add outdoor blinds below an awning?
Yes. Many homes combine a solid awning roof with drop-down outdoor blinds on the front or sides for extra wind and rain control.
