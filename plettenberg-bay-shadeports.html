Why use shadeports in Plett

Long hours of UV and salt-laden air age paintwork fast. Shadeports add a cooler, protected parking bay where structures or garages are limited.

  • Help prevent paint and dash fading
  • Reduce heat build-up in parked vehicles
  • Offer some hail and bird-drop protection
  • Can be more economical than full carports

Fabric and frame considerations

Coastal installations need proper galvanising, coating and fabric tensioning.

  • Look for coastal-rated steelwork and fixings
  • Ask about fabric UV rating and expected lifespan
  • Ensure posts and footings are engineered for wind loads
