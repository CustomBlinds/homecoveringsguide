Why Plett homes choose shutters

Shutters suit Plettenberg Bay because they handle sun and moisture better than many fabrics, add presence from the street and simplify cleaning in busy holiday homes.

  • Strong, simple lines that suit modern coastal architecture
  • Good light control without heavy drapery
  • Less dust collection than layered curtains
  • Security shutters add a clean alternative to bars

PVC indoor shutters

PVC indoor shutters are designed for wet and sunny spaces. They are popular in bathrooms, kitchens and sea-facing living areas.

  • Moisture tolerant for coastal humidity and steam
  • White and neutral finishes that suit most interiors
  • Great on stack doors where you want framed views

Security shutters

Security shutters combine a shutter look with a lockable aluminium or steel core. They are often used where owners would like both privacy and real security.

  • Lockable systems for doors and windows
  • Help replace traditional burglar bars
  • Work well on sea-facing sliders and bedroom doors

FAQ: Shutters in Plettenberg Bay

Will shutters warp near the sea?
Good quality PVC indoor shutters are built for humidity and sun. Security shutters use aluminium which is powder-coated for coastal conditions.
Can shutters replace security bars?
Security shutters are designed exactly for this purpose. Always confirm the security rating and locking system with your installer.
Are shutters easy to clean?
Yes. Most owners clean with a soft cloth and mild cleaner. Panels can be opened for deeper cleaning on both sides.
