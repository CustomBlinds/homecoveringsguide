What Pretoria homes need from a blind

Pretoria runs hot, noticeably warmer than Johannesburg, so the blind question here is as much about heat as light. This is where honeycomb (cellular) blinds make the strongest case: their air-pocket structure cuts heat transfer at the glass and keeps rooms cooler through the long Pretoria summer, which takes load off air conditioning. For sun-facing rooms, pairing a sunscreen roller for the day with a blockout for sleep handles both glare and the early sunrise.
