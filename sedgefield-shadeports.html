  • Reduce UV damage to paintwork and dashboards.

  • Provide a neater look for visitor parking in complexes and guesthouses.

Shade cloth versus PVC or solid coverings

Shade cloth structures

Shade cloth is breathable and ideal where the main aim is to reduce heat and sun, and where occasional fine rain is acceptable.

PVC or solid roof options

PVC and solid coverings give better rain protection, which can be important for permanent residents or for boats and caravans. They may need more careful design for wind and drainage.

Questions Sedgefield owners often ask

Will a shade port affect my neighbour or street view?

Height, colour and placement matter. Lower structures near the street or side yard usually have less impact on views from living spaces. Discuss this with your installer and check any complex or municipal guidelines before building.

Can I use one structure for cars, caravans and boats?

Yes, as long as the design allows for the highest and widest item and is engineered for the load and wind exposure. Make sure your installer knows all the uses you have in mind before finalising the design.
