What LockLatch style safety latches do

A LockLatch style device is a compact stainless steel latch that fixes between the frame and sash of a window or door. It creates a lockable gap so you can leave openings ajar without leaving them vulnerable. The gap is adjustable, which means you can set it small for security or slightly wider for airflow and pets.

In practice, this turns a fully open window or door into a controlled opening: air and light still move, but the opening cannot be pushed wider without unlocking the latch.

Frequently asked questions about LockLatch in George

Will LockLatch work on my aluminium windows and sliding doors?

In most cases yes. The system is designed to fit common aluminium and timber frames. A site check will confirm exactly where it can be installed and how wide the safe opening can be.

Can a safety latch help with baboons and monkeys?

A properly installed latch limits how far a window or door can open. This makes it significantly harder for baboons and other wildlife to push openings wide enough to enter, especially when combined with strong screens.

Is LockLatch useful for holiday homes in George?

Yes. Owners can leave selected openings set to a safe, lockable gap so the house breathes between stays. Guests also benefit from secure night ventilation while they sleep.

Does LockLatch replace burglar bars or security screens?

No. Think of it as a complement rather than a replacement. LockLatch controls the opening, while security screens or shutters add an additional protective layer where needed.
