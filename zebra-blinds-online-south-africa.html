Zebra blinds online in South Africa

Zebra blinds, also called double roller blinds, layer alternating sheer and solid bands on a single roller. Shifting the bands in or out of alignment moves you from a clear view, to soft filtered light, to privacy, without fully closing the blind. They suit multi-use rooms and open-plan spaces where the light need changes through the day. This guide explains how they work and how they compare to a duo roller.

What to check before you order online

  • The blind is made to your exact measurements, not a fixed size.
  • It is manufactured in South Africa, so a remake is quick if needed.
  • There is a genuine fit guarantee covering a measuring slip.
  • Pricing is shown live as you enter your sizes, with free nationwide delivery.
