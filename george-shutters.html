Maintenance and lifespan Shutters do not need intense maintenance, but they do best with light routine care: Dust blades lightly every few weeks, especially near braai areas. Rinse seaside openings occasionally with fresh water to remove salt build up. Check locks and hinges yearly so they keep closing smoothly.
